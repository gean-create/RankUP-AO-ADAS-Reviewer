Potential assessment · Part 3

Interview Practice

The Behavioral Events Interview (BEI) is built on one idea: past behavior predicts future performance. The panel isn't looking for a perfect person — they're listening for a real story told clearly. Structure your answers with S.T.A.R.:

S
Situation — briefly set the scene: where, when, who was involved
T
Task — what exactly you were responsible for in that moment
A
Action — the specific steps you personally took
R
Result — the outcome, and what you learned from it

Before you walk in

Final reminders

This reviewer is an independent study aid for AO II and ADAS applicants nationwide and is not an official DepEd publication. Point allocations follow DepEd Order No. 7, s. 2023 (Enclosure No. 5, "Criteria and Point System for Hiring and Promotion to Positions"), and qualification standards follow the CSC-approved QS for each position and salary grade. Exact schedules, exam formats, and local requirements are set by each Schools Division Office's Human Resource Merit Promotion and Selection Board (HRMPSB) — always confirm current announcements on your own SDO's official website or Facebook page before your assessment date.
